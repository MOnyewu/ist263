The Process

There are many ways to solve a Rubik’s Cube but on this page, we will go over the Beginner’s Method.

Section 1: The Cross(White Side)

The goal is to have the cross solved on the white side, with each edge color matching the color of the center piece.

Section 2: White Side Corner Pieces

The goal is to have the corner pieces inserted with both colors matching that of the edge pieces that it is touching.

Secction 3: Middle Layer Edges

The goal is to have the edge pieces on the middle layer inserted into the correct position with the colors matching on both sides.

Section 4: OLL(Orient Last Layer)

Yellow Cross

The goal is to have the yellow cross solved on the yellow side and the edge piece colors on the side do not matter at this point.

Yellow Top-Side Corner Pieces

The goal is to have the entire yellow side solved without caring about the orientation of the corner piece side colors.

Section 5: PLL(Permute the Last Layer)

Solve Yellow Corner Pieces

The goal is to have the corner piece colors (besides yellow) match with the center pieces on that face.

Solve Yellow Edges

The goal is to have the cube solved.
