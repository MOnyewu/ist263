The Beginning

Invented in 1974 by Erno Rubik, also originally called the Magic Cube. It was licensed by Ideal Toy Corp in 1980. By January 2009, 350 million cubes were sold world-wide.

Notation

The Rubikâ€™s Cube has six faces, three different kinds of pieces and 6 colors. The six colors are white, yellow, green, blue, orange, and red. White is opposite yellow, green is opposite blue, and orange is opposite red

The three different kinds of pieces are edge, corner, and center pieces. There are six center pieces, one for each side that denote which color that face should be. There are eight corner pieces, each having three colors (or faces). Finally, there are twelve edge pieces, each having two colors
